How to Join?

  • We do Device A = ppt in laptop (Agenda, Notes, HW) and Device B = Paper Book
  • Join with Device A (Laptop): Use your computer to join the meeting for audio, video, and to share your screen (slides, documents, etc.).
  • Join with Device B (Tablet/iPad): Join the same meeting using a tablet or second computer.
  • Use Companion Mode: On the second device, select "Use Companion Mode" when joining. This is critical as it automatically turns off microphone and speakers to prevent echo.
  • Share Whiteboard: On the tablet, open a writing app (like Jamboard, Google Keep, or a note-taking app) and click "Present" or "Share Screen".

Result: Participants will see your presentation from Device A and your handwritten notes from Device B simultaneously.
